Your home should be insured for its full replacement cost, not market value. We recommend enough dwelling coverage to rebuild your home at current construction costs. Personal property should be 50-70% of dwelling coverage. Liability coverage typically starts at $100,000, but we recommend $300,000-$500,000 for most homeowners. Our agents will help you calculate exact needs based on your home's construction, location, and personal assets.

Standard homeowners insurance does NOT cover flood damage. If you have a mortgage from a federally-backed lender and live in a high-risk flood zone, flood insurance is required. However, Florida's flood risk is high even in moderate-risk zones — 1 in 4 flood claims come from low-to-moderate risk areas. We highly recommend flood insurance for all Florida homeowners, especially given the state's extensive coastline and storm surge risk.
